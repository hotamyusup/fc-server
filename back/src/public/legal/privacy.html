CFP's Firecloud Privacy Policy

Last updated: [December 2018]

Information that is gathered from visitors

In common with other applications and websites, log files are stored on the app/web server saving details such as the visitor's IP address, device type (for app), browser type (web), referring page (web) and time of visit. Stored app content (device database) and/or Cookies may be used to remember visitor preferences when interacting with the application or website. Where registration is required, the visitor's email and a username/password will be stored on the server. A company phone number will also be stored when applicable.

How the Information is used

The information is used to enhance the vistor's experience when using the application or website to display client content. E-mail addresses will not be sold, rented or leased to 3rd parties. E-mail may be sent to inform you of news of our services or offers by us.

Visitor Options

You may be able to block cookies via your browser settings but this may prevent you from access to certain features of the website.

Cookies (web admin only)

Cookies are small digital signature files that are stored by your web browser that allow your preferences to be recorded when visiting the website. Also they may be used to track your return visits to the website.

We do not use 3rd party advertising companies.
